30/09/2016 - UAV Challenge 2018

Out goal is to compete in the 2018 Australian Outback UAV Challenge. The task is to autonomously find a 'farmer' wearing blue jeans in a field 20km from the starting point. The UAV must then land, accept a vial of 'blood' from the farmer and then take off again, returning to its starting point. All of this must be completed in 1 hour, and with no human interaction.

We deciding on parts and the design of the UAV. With 3 seperate teams, Physical design, Electrical design and Programming, we are able to seperate each task such that the progress of one does not affect the progress of the others.

30/09/2016 - Glider restoration

We have recently acquired a broken gilder (ASW 15) and plan to restore it as an acrobatic glider. As we only have the main fuselage, this will require fabricating new wing and tail sections.

There is also a plan of retrofitting and electric motor, making the glider self launching
